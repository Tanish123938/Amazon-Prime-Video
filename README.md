# Amazon Prime Video Dashboard (Power BI)
## Project Overview

This project analyzes Amazon Prime Video content data to understand trends in movies and TV shows based on genre, release year, country, ratings, and duration.
The dashboard provides an interactive way to explore how content is distributed and how the platform has evolved over time.

## Objectives

- Analyze the distribution of Movies vs TV Shows
- Identify top genres available on Amazon Prime
- Understand content growth by year
- Analyze country-wise content availability
- Explore ratings and duration patterns

## Tools & Technologies Used

Power BI – Data visualization & dashboarding

CSV Dataset – Data source

Power Query – Data cleaning & transformation

DAX – Measures and calculated columns

## Key Insights

- Movies make up a larger portion of Prime Video content
- Content additions increased significantly after 2015
- Drama and Comedy are the most common genres
- The majority of content originates from the United States and India
- TV shows generally have longer engagement duration than movies

## Conclusion

This dashboard helps users quickly understand Amazon Prime Video’s content library, trends, and diversity, making it useful for content analysis and strategic decision-making.
